Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Policy explains how the Kadie.AI Discord bot and website (“Service”) collect, use, and share information.

Who we are

Operator: Koshie Studios. Contact: support@koshie-studios.com.

Information we collect

  • Discord identifiers: user, guild, channel, role, and message IDs; usernames as needed.
  • Content you submit: forum posts, replies, blueprint names and definitions, configuration values.
  • Operational logs: timestamps, command usage, error logs, and basic analytics.
  • Website data: standard HTTP logs and essential cookies for sign-in and preferences.

How we use information

  • Operate bot features, blueprints, forums, and related APIs.
  • Security, abuse prevention, debugging, and improvement.
  • Compliance with law.

Sharing

  • With Discord to run bot features you invoke.
  • With service providers for hosting, storage, and monitoring (e.g., Firebase/Google Cloud, Railway, CDN). They act under our instructions.
  • For legal requests or to protect rights and safety.

Retention

Configuration persists until deleted. Operational logs are typically kept up to 90 days unless needed longer for security or legal reasons.

Security

Encryption in transit, access control, least-privilege service accounts, and audit logging. No system is perfectly secure.

Your choices and rights

  • Remove the bot from a guild to stop bot-side collection there.
  • Delete forum posts or blueprints via the UI where available.
  • Request access or deletion via support@koshie-studios.com (include your Discord ID).
  • EEA/UK/California users may have additional rights under GDPR/CCPA.

Children

Service is for users 13+ or the minimum age in your region.

International transfers

Data may be processed in the United States or other countries with appropriate safeguards where required.

Cookies and ads

We use essential cookies. If we show Google AdSense, Google and partners may use cookies/identifiers as described in our Cookie Policy and Google’s notices.

Changes

We may update this Policy and post the new date here.
